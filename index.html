Let's learn how to do the Whispering Queen

What you need

  • Cards
  • Hands
  • A spectator or audience

How to perform the tricks

  1. Tell the spectaor(s) that you need to find a specific queen in the deck
  2. "Accidentally" skip the queen, and make sure to look at the top card in the deck without them noticing
  3. then go back through and find the queen
  4. tell the spectator(s) that you don't do anything for this trick, but the queen is the one who does the magic
  5. The queen then tells you to split the pile in three piles. Make sure to keep track of which pile has the top card on it that you already saw
  6. The queen then tells you to ask the spectator to point to one of the piles
  7. Audience goes "WOW!"
